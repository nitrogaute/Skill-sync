# Ali Abdaal Frameworks

## Easy Mode Principles

Source: Ali Abdaal — How to Get Rich on Easy Mode

- **The one rule:** Help other people make money. B2B crushes B2C because businesses buy with spreadsheets, not emotions.
- **Tie your work to revenue.** The closer the line between what you do and money generated, the more you earn. Sales > support > admin.
- **Sell to people with money.** $50k service to a $5M business is easier than $50 product to a $40k earner. They care about ROI, not price tags.
- **Sweet spot:** $2k-$20k services where the outcome = "I help you make more money."
- **Price on value, not time.** Charge ~1/10 of value created. $100k value = $10k fee. Client gets a bargain, you get rich.
- **Build skills close to the money.** Sales, marketing, copywriting that ties to revenue > creative/technical skills disconnected from revenue.
- **The gap:** You're already helping someone make money (that's employment). The difference between value you create and your compensation is their profit margin — and it's bigger than you think.

## Idea Generation

Source: Ali Abdaal — How to Systematically Generate Profitable Business Ideas

- **Holy Trinity:** Every business needs (1) a person with money, (2) a problem they need solved, (3) a product/service that solves it. Start with person + problem, NOT the product.
- **Three phases:** Diverge (generate many ideas) → Converge (filter ruthlessly) → Emerge (discover through experimentation).
- **Diverge:** Map your craft skills (existing expertise), passions, and skills you want to learn. Then identify specific people you know who have problems you could solve.
- **Converge filter:** (1) Do I like working with these people? (2) Can I actually help them? (3) Will they be delighted to pay?
- **Start high-ticket ($2k+).** Target the premium 9% of buyers, not mass market. Same problem = vastly different price depending on WHO you serve (med school tutoring: £50 to UK families vs $5-15k to wealthy Chinese parents).
- **Know your first client by name.** If you can't name a specific person you'd sell to, the niche is too abstract.
- **2-year test:** Which niche could you work in for 2 years without burning out?
- **No-fail scenario:** If success was guaranteed, which business would you choose? (Reveals genuine excitement vs. safe bets.)

## Freedom Design

Source: Ali Abdaal — Financial Freedom Workshop (Hong Kong)

- **Five F's Freedom Profile:** Fun, Flexibility, Fulfillment, Finances → Freedom. Every income vehicle has a different profile. Choose based on desired life, not max revenue.
- **Time freedom ≠ financial freedom.** Time freedom is the journey, financial freedom is the destination. You can have 90% time freedom long before financial freedom.
- **Leaving money on the table = the other half of freedom.** Some freedom comes from earning. The rest comes from deciding enough is enough.
- **Diminishing returns of success.** 5,000 subscribers = peak feeling. 6.5M feels the same. After 6-12 months, money stops motivating. What lasts: **connection** (team, community) + **contribution** (tangible impact).
- **6-week validation rule.** Per idea: find person with money + painful problem → try to get a deposit in 6 weeks. No signal = scrap. Abdaal failed 7 times, #8 worked.
- **Vehicle matters more than effort.** Remote dev @ $10k/mnd with freedom > kebab van owner working 18hr days. Pick the right vehicle.
- **GPS for clarity.** Vague goal = vague plans. Abdaal's $1,500/hr coach focuses almost entirely on clarifying "what do you actually want?"
- **Abdaal's personal target:** £2M/yr profit lifestyle business. Not £10M. Deliberately capped.

## Starting & Sustaining

Source: Ali Abdaal — Financial Freedom Workshop

- **Financial freedom = skills, not luck.** "What order of keys to press on your keyboard." Learnable, not innate.
- **Four S's:** Strategy → Skills → **Starting** → **Sustaining**. Most people have enough strategy/skills but never start or quit too early.
- **Sales = only 3 methods:** (1) Content (magnetic marketing), (2) Ads, (3) 1-to-1 outreach. Plus email as support. That's it.
- **"Almost any niche can get you to six figures."** Stop overthinking the niche. Pick and commit.
- **Accountability squads:** 3-4 people with similar goals, standing meetings. Almost every successful creator has one. Fragile early — requires commitment.
- **Info diet:** Replace music with entrepreneur podcasts/audiobooks during commute. Surround yourself with the right voices.
- **"Fuck your feelings, follow the plan."** Negative thoughts are cars passing by. Observe, don't chase.
- **Reframe:** Every coffee shop, yoga teacher, accountant = £100k+ revenue. Business is normal, not extraordinary.
