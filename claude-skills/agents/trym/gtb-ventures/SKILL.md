# GTB Ventures — Business Playbook

Strategic knowledge base for GTB Ventures AS. Accumulates learnings, frameworks, and principles for evaluating and building ventures.

## Quick Reference

- **Entity:** GTB Ventures AS (holding company, funded Jan 2026)
- **Target:** 20k+ NOK/mnd autonomous revenue, Trym runs 90%+
- **Criteria:** Saves time, saves money, or makes money
- **Owner:** Gaute — CTO/VP Tech Dev, maritime/offshore background

## Decision Framework

When evaluating an opportunity, check against these filters in order:

1. **Autonomy test:** Can Trym (AI) run 90%+ of operations?
2. **Revenue model:** Recurring > one-time. Tiered pricing > flat.
3. **Customer segment:** B2B/premium > mass market
4. **Moat:** What stops competitors? Domain expertise, data, integrations?
5. **Time-to-revenue:** Weeks, not months. MVP-first.
6. **Gaute's edge:** Does it leverage his technical/maritime/energy background?

## Core Principles (distilled)

### Pricing & Customers
- Sell to wealthy / B2B first. Top 10% control 69% of wealth.
- Price on value (~1/10 of value created), not time.
- £1k-£10k first business sweet spot, 10-100 customers.
- Pricing pyramid: focus on the 10 who have 60% of budget, not the 90.
→ Deep dive: `references/hormozi-frameworks.md`

### Idea Generation & Validation
- Holy Trinity: person with money + painful problem + solution. Start with person+problem, NOT product.
- 6-week validation: try to get a deposit. No signal = scrap.
- Hormozi filter: painful + purchasing power + easy to target + growing market.
- Know your first client by name.
→ Deep dive: `references/abdaal-frameworks.md`

### Business Model
- Software = slowest start, infinite scale, 90%+ margins.
- Service = easiest start, talent-constrained. Systemize delivery.
- Match your personality to the model. Don't fight the shape.
- Sales = only 3 methods: content, ads, 1-to-1 outreach.
→ Deep dive: `references/hormozi-frameworks.md`

### Lifestyle Business Design
- Design business around desired life, not max revenue.
- 3-12 people = sweet spot. >13 = complexity explosion.
- Speed limit, not speed target. Consistency > quick wins.
- Stop, Stay, Start audit regularly.
- Five F's: Fun, Flexibility, Fulfillment, Finances → Freedom.
→ Deep dive: `references/priestley-ducker.md`

### Founder Mindset
- Focus = what you say NO to. One thing until it works.
- "Fuck your feelings, follow the plan."
- Bias to action. Reading/watching ≠ progress.
- Accountability squads: 3-4 people, standing meetings.
- "The magic you're looking for is in the work you're avoiding."
→ Deep dive: `references/hormozi-frameworks.md`, `references/abdaal-frameworks.md`

### Finance & Unit Economics
- Gross margin = product health. Profit margin = operational health. Both together = full picture.
- SaaS: 70-90% gross margins. CPG: 20-40%. Know your type.
- Buffett's 5 balance sheet rules: cash > debt, D/E < 0.8, no preferred stock, growing retained earnings, buybacks.
- 3-statement model (P&L + Balance Sheet + Cash Flow) = the language of business.
→ Deep dive: `references/finance-frameworks.md`

### Sales & Negotiation
- Sales = change management. Help clients move from current → desired state.
- SALES framework: Serve, Ask, Listen, Empathize, Summarize.
- CLOSER framework: Clarify, Label, Overview, Sell vacation, Explain, Reinforce.
- Always have a BATNA. Anchor high. Use silence.
- Proof > promises. Testimonials at peak satisfaction.
→ Deep dive: `references/sales-negotiation.md`

### Strategy & Disruption
- Value chain decoupling: find weakest link in customer journey → steal it from incumbents.
- 3 types: value creating (best), value eroding, value capturing.
- AI opportunity = reduce cost/time/effort at specific weak links, not spray everywhere.
→ Deep dive: `references/operations-tactics.md`

### Operations & AI Agents
- Create jobs for agents, not tasks. Recurring responsibilities.
- Cold outreach machine: systematizable by Trym.
- VA playbook: $300-500/mnd for execution after system proven.
- Founder's Triangle: Domain + Depth + Distribution. All three needed.
- DREAM Machine: Demand, Revenue, Engine, Admin, Marketing — AI automates all 5.
→ Deep dive: `references/operations-tactics.md`
