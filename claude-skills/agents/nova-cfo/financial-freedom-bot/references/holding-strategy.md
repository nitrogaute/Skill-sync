# Holding Company Strategy — GTB Ventures/Holding

Reference for holding company financial decisions. Read the current state from `04-Areas/GTB Ventures.md` before advising.

## Fritaksmetoden (Participation Exemption)

The core tax advantage of a Norwegian holding company:

- **Between companies**: Dividends and capital gains are 97% tax-exempt (3% included in income = 0.66% effective tax)
- **>90% ownership**: 100% tax-exempt (no 3% inclusion)
- **Losses**: NOT deductible under fritaksmetoden (asymmetric — gains exempt, losses not deductible)
- **Scope**: Applies to shares in EEA companies. Shares outside EEA with <10% ownership held <2 years are excluded.

**Practical implication**: Keep investment gains inside the holding company. Only extract to personal when needed for consumption. Every extraction triggers personal tax.

## Dividend vs Salary Optimization

The key question: how to extract money from the holding company.

**Salary from holding:**
- Subject to employer's AGA (arbeidsgiveravgift): 14.1% in sone 1 (Stavanger)
- Plus 5% finansskatt on salary from financial companies
- Builds pensjonsopptjening up to 7.1G
- Deductible for the company

**Styrehonorar (board fees):**
- Always personal income (cannot be invoiced via another AS)
- No finansskatt
- Builds pensjonsopptjening
- Keep reasonable (50-100k) and document actual board work

**Dividend:**
- 37.84% effective personal tax (after oppjusteringsfaktor)
- Reduced by skjermingsfradrag (risk-free rate * share value)
- No AGA for the company
- Does NOT build pensjonsopptjening

**Crossover point in AGA sone 1 (Stavanger):** ~850,000 NOK salary
- Below this: salary is more efficient (builds pension, reasonable marginal rate)
- Above this: dividend is more efficient (no AGA, lower combined rate than trinnskatt trinn 4+)
- Optimal: salary up to ~850k for pensjonspoeng, dividend above that

## Reinvestment vs Personal Extraction

**Keep in holding when:**
- Reinvesting in new ventures or shares (fritaksmetoden protects gains)
- Building war chest for property purchase through the company
- Formuesskatt planning (double verdsettelsesrabatt: company level + share level)

**Extract personally when:**
- Needed for personal consumption or mortgage qualification
- Leveraging skjermingsfradrag before it resets
- Tax rate arbitrage is favorable in current year

## Investment Evaluation Framework

When GTB Ventures evaluates an investment opportunity:

1. **Fit check**: Does it align with holding company purpose and competence?
2. **Fritaksmetoden eligibility**: Is the investee an EEA company? Will ownership exceed key thresholds?
3. **Due diligence**: Financials, team, market, legal
4. **Portfolio impact**: Concentration risk, total exposure, liquidity
5. **Tax efficiency**: Structure the investment for maximum fritaksmetoden benefit
6. **Exit horizon**: When and how will returns be realized?
7. **FI contribution**: Does this accelerate or delay the path to financial freedom?
