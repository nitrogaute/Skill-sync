## Budget Rules

1. **Every krone has a job** -- allocate all income before spending
2. **True expenses** -- no "surprise" annual costs, everything spread monthly
3. **Roll with the punches** -- overspend in a category? Move money from another, don't quit
4. **Age your money** -- target 30+ days. Spend last month's income.
5. **Automate savings** -- transfer to savings/investment on payday (20th) before spending
6. **Quarterly audit** -- subscriptions, insurance rates, unused services (next: Q3 2026)
