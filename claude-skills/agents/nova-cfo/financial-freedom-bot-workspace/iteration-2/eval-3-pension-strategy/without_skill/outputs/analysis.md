# Pension Strategy Analysis

**Profile:** Age 35, ~1.5M NOK at KM, AFP eligible, 15K/year IPS.

## The Four Pillars

### Folkepensjon
- Full rights at 40 years. At 67 (estimated): ~280-320K/year. At 62 (reduced): ~200-220K.

### OTP via KM
- Estimated 5-7% contribution. Annual employer contribution: ~69-96K.
- Projected at 62 (27yr, 5% real): 3.8-5.3M. Drawn over 10-15 years.
- **Action:** Switch to 100% equity index fund.

### AFP
- Lifelong supplement from 62: ~80-120K/year.
- **Eligibility:** 7 of last 9 years before 62 in AFP-covered employment.
- Lifetime value: ~2.3M+. Equivalent to ~2.5-3M portfolio.
- **If you leave KM before 55, you risk losing AFP.**

### IPS
- Current: 15K/year. **New max 2026: 25,000.** Tax deduction: 5,500/year.
- Formuesskatt: 100% exempt.
- At 25K/year, 27yr, 5% real: ~1.5M.
- **Always max it — interest-free loan from the state.**

## Pension + FI: The Bridge Strategy

Without pension: FI number at 3.5% SWR = ~17M (at 600K expenses).
With pension at 62: covers ~620-780K/year. **Adjusted FI number at 62: ~0.**

**Your challenge is a bridge problem:**
- FI at 50: need ~7.2M bridge capital (12 years)
- FI at 55: need ~3.8M bridge capital (7 years)
- At 62, pension takes over.

## Recommendations

| Action | Priority | Impact |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Increase IPS to 25K/year | High — Immediate | +2,200/yr tax, +825K more at 62 |
| Optimize OTP fund (100% equity) | High — This week | +1-2M at 62 |
| Protect AFP eligibility | Critical — Ongoing | ~2.5M lifetime value |
| Annual nav.no pension check | Medium | Catches errors |

**AFP constraint:** Leaving KM for self-employment (GTB Ventures) before ~55 carries a hidden cost of ~2.5M in lifetime pension.
