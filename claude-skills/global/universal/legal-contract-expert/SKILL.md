# Legal Contract Expert - Green Hydrogen Supply Chain

Expert guidance for business collaboration agreements under Norwegian law.

## Quick Reference: Agreement Lifecycle

```
NDA → LOI → Collaboration Agreement → Partnership Agreement → M&A
│      │           │                        │                  │
│      │           │                        │                  └─ Full integration
│      │           │                        └─ Joint venture/equity
│      │           └─ Project-based cooperation
│      └─ Non-binding intent (except exclusivity/confidentiality)
└─ Protect confidential information during discussions
```

## Core Workflow

### 1. Initial Assessment

Before any contract task:
1. Identify agreement type and stage in partnership lifecycle
2. Determine parties' relative maturity (startup vs. established)
3. Check for supporting documents (ask user if not provided):
   - Company standard terms
   - Previous agreements between parties
   - Board mandates or approval limits
   - Industry-specific requirements

### 2. Document Analysis

When reviewing contracts:
1. Extract key commercial terms (see [key-terms.md](references/key-terms.md))
2. Identify risk allocation provisions
3. Flag deviations from market standard
4. Check Norwegian law compliance
5. Note Green Hydrogen sector-specific concerns

### 3. Risk Evaluation

Apply risk matrix:
- **Critical**: Unlimited liability, broad indemnities, IP assignment
- **High**: Exclusivity without termination, change of control triggers
- **Medium**: Non-standard warranty periods, audit rights
- **Low**: Administrative provisions, notice periods

### 4. Output Deliverables

Provide structured analysis:
```
## Executive Summary
[2-3 sentence overview]

## Key Terms
[Table of critical provisions]

## Risk Assessment
[Categorized findings]

## Recommendations
[Prioritized action items]

## Negotiation Points
[Suggested positions with fallbacks]
```

## Norwegian Law Framework

See [references/norwegian-law.md](references/norwegian-law.md) for detailed guidance.

Key principles:
- **Avtaleloven** (Contracts Act): Formation, interpretation, invalidity
- **Kjøpsloven** (Sale of Goods Act): Default rules for sales
- **Loyalty principle**: Duty of good faith in negotiations and performance
- **Background law**: Contracts interpreted against mandatory rules

Default rules (apply unless contracted out):
- No limitation of liability for gross negligence or willful misconduct
- Reasonable notice for termination of indefinite agreements
- Force majeure generally recognized but define specifically

## Green Hydrogen Sector Considerations

See [references/hydrogen-sector.md](references/hydrogen-sector.md) for industry specifics.

Key concerns:
- **Technology risk**: Electrolyzer performance guarantees, degradation curves
- **Offtake agreements**: Volume commitments, pricing mechanisms (fixed vs. indexed)
- **Regulatory compliance**: EU Taxonomy, additionality requirements, GO certificates
- **Supply chain**: Equipment delivery, construction milestones, LDs
- **Certification**: Origin guarantees, carbon intensity thresholds

## Maturity Imbalance Considerations

When parties have different maturity levels:

| Issue | Startup Position | Established Company Position |
|-------|------------------|------------------------------|
| Liability caps | Cap at contract value or insurance | Seek higher caps or carve-outs |
| Payment terms | Milestone-based, advances | Payment on delivery/acceptance |
| IP | Retain background IP, share foreground | Seek broad licenses or assignment |
| Termination | Longer notice, termination fees | Flexibility, termination for convenience |
| Guarantees | Resist parent guarantees | Require security or guarantees |
