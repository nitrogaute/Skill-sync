# Portfolio Concentration Analysis: KOG/KM on ASK

## Your Situation
- ~1.5M NOK in KOG/KM, 85% of portfolio, on ASK
- You work at Kongsberg Maritime — double exposure (human capital correlation)

## Should You Diversify? Yes.

85% is extreme concentration. You are taking uncompensated risk — the market doesn't reward company-specific risk. Target: reduce to 20-30% over 1-3 years.

The killer risk: if KM hits hard times, your salary, bonus, OTP pension, and 85% of investments all decline simultaneously.

## How to Diversify Tax-Efficiently

**Inside ASK = zero tax.** Sell KOG/KM and buy index funds within the same ASK account — no capital gains tax triggered. Tax only applies on cash withdrawals from ASK above your total deposits (37.84% on gains, reduced by skjermingsfradrag).

Plan: Sell 15-20% of position every 2-3 months. Buy KLP AksjeGlobal Indeks or similar low-cost global fund. Direct all new savings to index funds.

## FI Impact

At ~1.76M total, 3.5% SWR: supports ~62K/year — far from FI. A 50% concentrated drawdown sets FI back 7-10 years. Diversified compounding at ~7% with steady contributions is more reliable.

| Scenario | Return | Volatility | FI Outlook |
|----------|--------|-----------|------------|
| 85% concentrated | ~10% (uncertain) | 35-45% | Fragile |
| 25% balanced | ~8-9% | 15-18% | Reliable |
| 100% index | ~7-8% | 15% | Most predictable |
