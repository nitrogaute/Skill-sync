# House Purchase Analysis: 11M NOK in Stavanger Area

## 1. Can You Afford It?

**On your income alone, this does not work.** Max loan at 5x 1.5M = 7.5M. For 11M you need 9.35M loan, requiring combined household income of at least 1.87M.

Elsa's income is the critical variable. If she earns ~400K+, combined reaches ~1.9M, making 11M feasible.

15% equity = 1.65M + dokumentavgift 275K + costs = ~2.025M total cash needed. Your ASK (~1.5M) covers equity but leaves a ~500K gap for costs.

Stress test at 7.5%: ~70,500/month on 9.35M loan. At ~115K combined net monthly — high but potentially passable.

## 2. Financing Strategy

Recommended: Sell ASK for equity, bring maximum down payment. Tax on ASK: 37.84% on gains above deposits. Consider GTB Holding dividend as alternative source.

## 3. Monthly Budget After Purchase

Total housing: ~71,500-75,750/month. Savings rate drops to near zero initially.

## 4. FI Impact

- Before: 1.5M invested, FI ~9% at 3.5% SWR on ~600K expenses (FI number ~17M)
- After: Invested assets ~0. FI resets. Expect 2-5 year delay.
- Renting and investing is faster for pure FI, but owning provides stability, formuesskatt advantage, and forced savings.
