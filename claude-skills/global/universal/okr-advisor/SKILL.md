# OKR Advisor

## The Formula

**"I will (Objective) as measured by (Key Results)."**

- **Objective**: Qualitative, inspirational, action-oriented, time-bound (quarterly), team-controlled
- **Key Results**: Measurable outcomes with specific targets. 3-5 per Objective. Must be verifiable pass/fail.

## Critical Distinction: Outcomes vs. Outputs

Outputs (avoid as KRs): "Launch feature X", "Ship release 2.0", "Hire 5 engineers"
Outcomes (use as KRs): "Increase adoption from 20% to 50%", "Reduce churn from 5% to 2%"

## OKR Types

| Type | Expected Score | Use When |
|------|---------------|----------|
| **Committed** | 1.0 (100%) | Delivery is critical to operations |
| **Aspirational** | 0.7 (70%) = success | Innovation/breakthrough goal |
| **Learning** | Based on learning | New markets, high uncertainty |

## Common Mistakes

1. **Tasks as KRs** - "Install package X" vs. "Reduce errors by 50%"
2. **BAU goals** - "Maintain satisfaction" vs. "Increase NPS from 32 to 45"
3. **Sandbagging** - Consistently scoring 1.0 means not ambitious enough
4. **Too many** - Max 3-5 Objectives, 3-5 KRs each
5. **No baseline** - "Improve response time" vs. "Reduce from 24h to 4h"
6. **OKRs as KPIs** - KPIs maintain health, OKRs drive change

## Tech KR Patterns

| Category | Pattern |
|----------|---------|
| Performance | Reduce [metric] from X to Y |
| Quality | Achieve [metric] of X |
| Adoption | Increase [usage] from X to Y |
| Efficiency | Improve [metric] by X% |
| Satisfaction | Achieve [score] of X |

## Alignment

```
Company OKR → Department OKR → Team OKR
```

Cross-dependent teams should co-create shared OKRs.

## Quarterly Cadence

- Week 1-2: Draft and align OKRs
- Weeks 3-12: Execute, track weekly (on track / at risk / behind)
- Week 13: Grade (Google 0.0-1.0 scale), reflect, plan next quarter

## Review Checklist

**Objectives**: Qualitative? Inspirational? Achievable in quarter? Aligned with strategy?
**Key Results**: Measurable number? Outcome not task? Baseline and target? Ambitious but achievable? Objectively verifiable?
**Set**: 3-5 KRs per O? 3-5 Os max? Mix of committed/aspirational? Dependencies identified?
