- Penalties: up to 7% global annual revenue
- Requirements: risk management, data governance, human oversight, technical documentation, conformity assessment
- MASS Code aligned with EU AI Act since August 2024
- 7-nation MOU for remote/autonomous vessel operations pathway

### Kongsberg Maritime Specifics

## Workflow

### When updating the strategy document:
1. Read current version at `docs/km-ai-strategi-forslag.md`
2. Research latest developments (web search for maritime AI, EU AI Act, Kongsberg, competitors)
3. Identify gaps or outdated information
4. Update with new data, maintaining the 5-pilar structure
5. Keep the executive tone -- no fluff, concrete benchmarks
6. Norwegian language, aa/oe/ae encoding for special characters

### When reviewing/critiquing:
1. Check factual accuracy of all benchmarks and references
2. Verify regulatory timelines (EU AI Act, IMO MASS, DNV)
3. Assess completeness: are key maritime AI trends covered?
4. Evaluate actionability: can leadership act on this?
5. Flag any missing risk factors
6. Provide specific improvement suggestions

### When preparing for presentation:
1. Extract key talking points (max 10)
2. Prepare counterargument responses
3. Identify likely leadership questions
4. Create one-page executive summary if needed
5. Suggest workshop format for pilot prioritization

## Review Flow

Three-stage review process:

### Stage 1: Factual Accuracy
- All market numbers sourced and current
- Regulatory timelines verified
- Competitor/benchmark examples accurate
- No outdated references

### Stage 2: Strategic Coherence
- Do the 5 pillars cover all critical areas?
- Is the implementation roadmap realistic?
- Are risk factors comprehensive?
- Does it align with KM's actual capabilities?

### Stage 3: Presentation Readiness
- Is it concise enough for leadership attention?
- Are recommendations actionable?
- Is the business case clear?
- Does it anticipate common objections?

## Output Format

Updates to the strategy doc should:
- Maintain existing structure unless restructuring is explicitly requested
- Use tables for comparisons and scorecards
- Include specific numbers/benchmarks (not vague claims)
- End each section with clear "tiltak" (actions)
- Keep total document under 200 lines
